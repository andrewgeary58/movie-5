Actors

-Brad Pitt
Brad Pitt plays Ladybug who is a veteran assassin, who seemingly never has luck and is trying to only accept small jobs that do not require killing.

-Joey King
Joey King as The Prince, a manipulative young assassin disguised as a schoolgirl, who seeks vengeance on the White Death.

-Aaron Taylor-Johnson and Brian Tyree Henry
Tangerine another British assassin played by Aaron Taylor-Johnson. Starring as Tangerine’s counterpart and partner in crime is Brian Tyree Henry as the assassin known as Lemon.

-Andrew Koji
Andrew Koji plays Yuichi Kimura, a Japanese assassin.

-Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio
The Wolf is another dangerous hitman on board the bullet train who has a personal ongoing feud with Ladybug. He is portrayed by the Latin megastar Bad Bunny.

-Michael Shannon
Michael Shannon is starring as the leader of a criminal organization who is another major antagonist Ladybug will have to face. He is the father of the Prince. His wife is thought to have been killed by Ladybug.
